# SNDK — Watchlist Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
SNDK is a freshly independent flash storage business with the steepest NAND margin expansion in history, benefiting from AI data storage demand — but currently too extended technically to enter.

## Why It's On the Watchlist
New spinoff from WD creates a pure-play NAND story. If margin expansion sustains, significant re-rating potential as the company builds its own public track record. Similar demand thesis to MU but earlier-stage.

## Entry Conditions (all should be met before initiating)
- RSI resets below 60 on a pullback
- Gross margins sustaining above 50% for two consecutive quarters
- NAND pricing environment stable (no aggressive Samsung/SK Hynix pricing cuts)
- Preferred entry zone: pullback to 5 EMA (~$1,112) or 20 EMA (~$732)

## What Would Remove It From Watchlist
- Gross margins compress (structural, not transient)
- Samsung or SK Hynix aggressive NAND pricing cuts materialise
- Post-spinoff governance or accounting concerns emerge
- 10% short interest proves correct — stock breaks down
