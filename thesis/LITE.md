# LITE — Watchlist Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
LITE makes the optical components that move data at terabit speeds inside AI data centres — a pure-play on AI networking buildout, S&P 500 member, with analyst targets of $1,000–$1,130 following Q3 earnings beat.

## Why It's On the Watchlist
Direct pick-and-shovel on AI data centre interconnects. Just beat Q3 earnings estimates. Multiple analyst target upgrades post-earnings ($1,000–$1,130). S&P 500 inclusion brings passive buying.

## Entry Conditions
- Post-earnings "sell the news" selling exhausts itself (wait 1–2 weeks minimum)
- RSI resets below 65
- Price stabilises and forms a base above the 5 EMA (~$914)
- Gross margins show improvement trajectory above 40% (currently 37%)

## What Would Remove It From Watchlist
- Post-earnings downtrend extends beyond 2 weeks — sustained selling after an earnings beat is a negative signal
- Gross margins fail to expand — structural margin limitation for optical components
- A competitor (COHR) wins a major hyperscaler design win expected to go to LITE

## Key Watch Item
LITE reported earnings and beat estimates, but the stock dropped — classic "sell the news." This is not necessarily bearish on the business, but it means the move already happened. Don't chase. Monitor price action over the next 2 weeks.
