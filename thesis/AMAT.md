# AMAT — Watchlist Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
AMAT is the indispensable equipment supplier to the entire semiconductor industry — AI-driven fab investment is extending the capex cycle, but the equipment order lag means revenue turns positive slightly after the chip demand recovery.

## Why It's On the Watchlist
Best-in-class semiconductor equipment franchise with ~20% global market share. Revenue slightly negative YoY now (equipment orders lag chip demand 6–12 months) but the cycle should turn. Most technically constructive watchlist setup — recently broke above 20 EMA with RSI 65 (not extreme).

## Entry Conditions
- Revenue growth turning positive (confirms equipment cycle has turned)
- Gross margin sustaining above 47%
- China export control risk not escalating further
- Preferred entry: pullback to 20 EMA (~$347) for better risk/reward; current ~$411 is acceptable given clean technical setup

## What Would Remove It From Watchlist
- China export controls expand significantly — AMAT loses largest market
- Revenue growth stays negative for two more quarters
- Equipment capex cycle turns negative (fab capex cuts — monitor Intel, TSMC, Samsung capex guidance)

## Near-Term Catalyst
CEO/CFO investor webcasts scheduled May–June 2026 — could provide forward guidance on equipment order trajectory.
