# APH — Investment Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
APH is the most defensive AI infrastructure play in the portfolio — every data centre, GPU cluster, and 5G network uses their connectors, giving steady compounding growth without the volatility of pure-play semis.

## Why I Hold It
Picks-and-shovels stability. APH has compounded earnings at ~15% for 20+ years through multiple tech cycles. AI capex adds a new growth vector to an already reliable business. Lower beta than NVDA/MU/MRVL — provides some stability to the AI value chain allocation.

## What Proves It's Working
- Organic revenue growth (ex-acquisitions) sustaining above 10%
- Operating margin holding above 25%
- AI/data centre segment growing as % of total revenue

## What Breaks It
- Organic revenue growth decelerates below 10% for two consecutive quarters
- Margin compression from the €1.1B debt issuance (May 2026) integration costs persists
- A major customer insources connector manufacturing

## Current Watch Item
APH is technically weak — 18% below its 52-week high while sector peers rally. The €1.1B debt issuance in May 2026 adds near-term leverage. Need to monitor whether this is temporary underperformance (thesis intact) or the market seeing something structural.

## Bear Case (read before adding)
At 40x P/E for a business growing organically at 10–15%, the multiple is premium and sensitive to any growth disappointment. The debt issuance adds leverage at a time when the stock is already underperforming. Technically the weakest chart in the AI value chain holdings.
