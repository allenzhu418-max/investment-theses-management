# NVDA — Investment Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
NVDA is the infrastructure layer of the AI era — its CUDA software moat and GPU dominance give it pricing power that justifies a premium multiple through the AI capex buildout cycle.

## Why I Hold It
No credible near-term competitor can replicate the CUDA software ecosystem. Every AI lab, hyperscaler, and enterprise trains on NVDA hardware. The switching cost is enormous — years of optimised code, libraries, and workflows lock customers in regardless of hardware price differences.

## What Proves It's Working
- Data centre revenue growing >50% YoY
- Gross margins holding above 70%
- Hyperscalers (Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Meta) continuing to guide capex increases each quarter

## What Breaks It
- Two consecutive quarters of gross margin compression below 65%
- A hyperscaler publicly shifting significant training workloads to AMD or custom silicon at scale
- US export controls expand to cut off remaining international markets materially

## Bear Case (read this when tempted to add)
NVDA's valuation prices in perfection. Any slowdown in AI capex — from recession, regulation, or LLM efficiency gains reducing GPU demand — reprices this 30–40% before fundamentals catch up. AMD is closer than the market thinks on software compatibility.
