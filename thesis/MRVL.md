# MRVL — Investment Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
MRVL is a custom silicon play — hyperscalers are designing their own AI chips and MRVL is the primary partner building them, giving it sticky, high-margin revenue independent of NVDA's dominance.

## Why I Hold It
The custom ASIC thesis is a partial hedge to NVDA — even in a world where hyperscalers reduce GPU purchases, they're increasing custom silicon spend, and MRVL benefits. Google's TPU, Amazon's Trainium — MRVL builds the networking fabric around all of them.

## What Proves It's Working
- Data infrastructure revenue growing >30% YoY
- New custom silicon programme announcements from additional hyperscaler clients
- Operating margins expanding toward 30%+ (currently 19%)

## What Breaks It
- A major hyperscaler cancels or significantly delays a custom silicon programme
- Two consecutive quarters of revenue growth below 15%
- NVDA successfully enters custom silicon market and displaces MRVL at a major client

## Bear Case (read before adding)
MRVL has essentially no revenue yet from custom silicon at scale — it's a forward expectation trade. If hyperscaler custom chip programmes face delays (they always do), or volumes disappoint, the multiple compresses sharply. At RSI 96 and 23% above analyst consensus, the stock already prices in near-perfect execution for several years.
