# COHR — Watchlist Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
COHR is a vertically integrated optical and laser components company at the intersection of AI networking and telecom — similar thesis to LITE but more diversified across end markets, with slightly less extreme technical extension.

## Why It's On the Watchlist
Broader application base than LITE (telecom, industrial, medical, defence — not just data centres). PEG 0.92 is reasonable for a company growing earnings 73% YoY. Less extended technically than LITE (RSI 75 vs 82.5).

## Entry Conditions
- RSI resets below 65
- Gross margins expanding consistently above 38% (currently 36%)
- Evidence of hyperscaler transceiver design wins
- Preferred entry: pullback to 5 EMA (~$322) or better

## What Would Remove It From Watchlist
- Gross margin compression (currently thin at 36%)
- Debt burden (D/E 40x) becomes a constraint if capital markets tighten
- LITE or other competitors dominate AI networking transceiver sockets at major hyperscalers

## LITE vs COHR Decision Framework
Monitor LITE's post-earnings price action to understand which has better near-term momentum. If LITE stabilises and recovers, it may be the stronger entry. If LITE's post-earnings selling accelerates, COHR becomes the preferred optical play.
