# WMT — Investment Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
WMT is compounding through the transition from a traditional retailer to a data/advertising/fulfilment platform — the business is more valuable than its retail margins suggest, and the premium P/E is justified if the mix shift continues.

## Why I Hold It
Defensive anchor with a credible growth narrative. WMT survived e-commerce disruption and is now becoming an advertising business (Walmart Connect) and fulfilment network. Defensive in downturns, growing in upturns. ~$1T market cap provides stability.

## What Proves It's Working
- Advertising and marketplace revenue growing >30% YoY
- E-commerce penetration as % of total sales increasing each quarter
- International (Flipkart, Walmex) showing sustained growth

## What Breaks It
- US comparable sales growth drops below 3% for two consecutive quarters
- Amazon takes meaningful grocery market share in WMT's core demographic
- Advertising revenue growth stalls — this is the justification for the premium multiple

## Bear Case (read before adding)
40x forward P/E for a retailer is expensive. WMT is priced like a tech company but operates like a retailer. Any execution miss or macro consumer spending slowdown reprices this quickly. The emerging surveillance pricing legislation is an early regulatory risk signal worth tracking.
