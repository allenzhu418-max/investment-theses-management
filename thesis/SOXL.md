# SOXL — Investment Thesis

## One-Sentence Thesis
SOXL is a tactical momentum instrument on the semis sector, providing 3x leveraged exposure when the sector has clear directional momentum — including indirect CPU exposure (Intel, AMD) not held individually.

## Why I Hold It
No individual CPU stocks in the portfolio. SOXL provides broad semis exposure including CPU names. When the semis sector trends up, SOXL amplifies the return. Currently the sector is in a strong uptrend (+29–35% MoM on SMH/SOXX).

## Important: This Is a Trade, Not an Investment
3x daily leverage means this is managed as a position with a defined exit, not held passively. The 3x rebalancing creates volatility decay — in a sideways or choppy market, SOXL loses value even if the sector ends flat.

## What Proves It's Working
- SMH and SOXX both in uptrend (above 20 EMA)
- Semis sector earnings cycle in expansion phase
- No signs of sector rotation out of semis

## What Breaks It (Exit Signals)
- SMH breaks below its 200-day MA on a weekly close — sector trend has turned
- Holding through a sideways/choppy market period — exit before volatility decay compounds
- Any factor that specifically hits broad semis (export controls, supply shock, valuation reset)

## Bear Case (read before adding)
In 2022, SOXL fell 90% while the underlying index fell ~40% — the leverage compounded the drawdown catastrophically. Holding through a down cycle without a stop is not a thesis, it's hope. Define your exit before the next add.
